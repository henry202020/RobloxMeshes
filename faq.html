Frequently asked questions

How do I import .rbxm files?

Simply drag the file to Roblox Studio/Workspace.

What are .rbxl and .rbxm files?

.rbxl files are games/experiences, and .rbxm files are models such as meshes, scripts, animations...

What are .rbxlx and .rbxmx files?

They are similar to .rbxl & .rbxm files, but in XML (text) format.
