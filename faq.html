Frequently asked questions

Do you need a page for frequently asked questions?

A good FAQ page is an important part of a knowledge base. You can conduct business without it, but having one helps save operational costs and makes it easier for customers to solve their own problems.

What are other ways to answer frequently asked questions?

You can use automated chatbots, create step-by-step video tutorials on YouTube, or build a community server (like Discord) where users can help each other and search past answers.

How do knowledge bases differ from FAQ pages?

An FAQ page focuses on quick, short answers to the most common questions. A knowledge base is a complete documentation system, containing long articles, user manuals, and detailed technical guides.

Why is integrating a search bar important for FAQ pages?

A search bar allows users to find their answers instantly without scrolling through dozens of questions, significantly improving the user experience on your site.
